I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University.  

Previously, I was a Visiting Faculty Researcher at Google. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the Foundations of Data Analystics Institute at University of California, Berkeley and was very fortunate to work with Michael Mahoney. Before that, I was a Research Fellow in the Foundations of Data Science program at the Simons Institute in Fall 2018. I graduated with my PhD from UT Austin. I am very fortunate to be able to learn from and interact with Professors Joydeep Ghosh, and Alex Dimakis. I am interested in various aspects of algorithmic machine learning, both from theory and practical viewpoints.
